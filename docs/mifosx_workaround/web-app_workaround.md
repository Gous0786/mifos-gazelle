## Workaround Videos

The following videos demonstrate the workaround for MifoX login issue.
   
<br>

***Safari Browser***

- Make sure you navigate to https://mifos.mifos.gazelle.test 

- When you get warning "This Connection is Not Private"

- Click on "Show Details"

- Then click on "visit this website"

- You will then able to login using mifos/password

- [VIDEO OF WORKAROUND](https://youtu.be/0wgBJGE4LX4)

<br>

***Chrome Browser***

- Make sure you navigate to https://mifos.mifos.gazelle.test 

- When you get warning "Your connection is not private"

- Click on "Advanced"

- Then click on "Proceed to mifos.mifos.gazelle.test (unsafe)"

- You will then be able to login using mifos/password

- [VIDEO OF WORKAROUND](https://youtu.be/n9m8Ihokzfg)

<BR>


***Firefox Browser***

- Make sure you navigate to https://mifos.mifos.gazelle.test 

- When you get "Warning: Potential Security Risk Ahead"

- Click on "Advanced"

- Then click on "Accept the Risk and Continue"

- You will then be able to login using mifos/password

- [VIDEO OF WORKAROUND](https://youtu.be/CZUXIFbVHVQ)
